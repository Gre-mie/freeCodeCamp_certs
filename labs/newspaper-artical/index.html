Digital Offerings

By Gremie Wednesday 30th October 2082
🍰

There was a time when everyone believed in fay beings whose mischievous antics were blamed for life's mishaps. It was common to leave offerings to appease these magical folk, hoping they might lend a hand around the home.

Now in this digital age, they have largely been forgotten. Yet when the internet fails we may find themselves "praying to the internet gods."

If you can't explain your technology's odd behaviour, perhaps it's time to leave a digital offering once more.

Gremlin favourites

  • 🍯 (Honey)

    Traditional but may result in stings during collection

  • 🍰 (Cake)

    Takes time to produce but forms a bond with the gremlin that receives it.

  • 🍬 (Candy)

    Appreciated but often leads to hyperactive gremlins
